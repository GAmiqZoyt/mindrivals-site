Delete your MindRivals account

App: MindRivals (com.mindrivals.app) · Developer contact: YOUR-EMAIL-HERE

You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data directly inside the app — no email or request form needed. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

How to delete your account

1. Open MindRivals on your device
2. Tap Profile (bottom of the home screen)
3. Scroll to the Account section
4. Tap Delete account and confirm

What gets deleted

Your account identity, username, avatar, cloud XP and level records, leaderboard entries, daily-challenge history, duel records, friend connections, and the history of questions served to you. This is everything MindRivals stores about you in the cloud.

What remains on your device

Offline progress stored locally on your phone (offline levels) is not part of your cloud account. Remove it by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

If you can no longer access the app

If the app is uninstalled and your account was secured with an email or Google sign-in, reinstall the app, log back in, and delete the account as described above. Otherwise, contact YOUR-EMAIL-HERE with your username and we will remove the account for you.
